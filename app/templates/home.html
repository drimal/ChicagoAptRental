Apartment Rental Price in Chicago

The data for this project was obtained by scraping 5k apartment rental listings in Chicago craigslist . The neighbourhood information scraped from the craigslist was incomplete so the latitude and longitude information was mapped to the neighborhoods. After some data wrangling and EDA, couple of tree based regressor were fitted. The final trained model is the gradient boosting regressor with R2 of 0.8 on the validation data.
